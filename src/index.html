Aerospace Orchestration Matrix

How VyomFix transforms unstructured engineering models into airworthy assets through Hybrid AI.

01

Digital Graph Integration

Our LLM-driven engine parses unstructured PLM data and Bill of Materials to construct a dynamic Knowledge Graph. This creates a high-fidelity digital twin that maps relationship dependencies between every fastener, sensor, and subsystem.

02

Cognitive Floor Orchestration

The platform synchronizes assembly tasks in real-time, assigning missions to work cells based on hardware telemetry and technician certification levels. This elimimates queuing bottlenecks via predictive throughput analysis.

03

Validation & Anomaly Shield

During integration, our ML models capture telemetry and photographic evidence to perform real-time anomaly detection. Every step generates a back-trackable decision tree, automatically populating the Regulatory Shield for airworthiness sign-off.
